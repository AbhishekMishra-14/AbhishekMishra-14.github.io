Overview - Kalinga Institute of Industrial Technology, Bhubaneswar

Kalinga Institute of Industrial Technology (KIIT), Bhubaneswar, India, established in 1997, was granted deemed to be University status (u/s 3 of UGC Act 1956) in 2004 by the Government of India.

As a university with a vision, KIIT promotes sports and games in a big way among the students. It has the most elaborate and comprehensive sports infrastructure located in a single place in the country. This includes a world-class multi-sport stadium and 16 international standard sports complexes distributed on different campuses.

Programs

  • Bachelor of Science in Computer Science
  • Bachelor of Fine Arts in Visual Arts
  • Bachelor of Science in Nursing
  • Bachelor of Science in Engineering
